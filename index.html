About Us

At BMHS, we are always keen to take on challenging jobs. We design and manufacture power packs as per customer requirements. We supply power packs for various applications such as machine tools, boiler applications, and steel and chemical plants through OEMs. We also manufacture filtration systems to maintain oil cleanliness at the desired level, as well as lubrication and cooling systems typically used in transmissions. Additionally, we offer repair and maintenance services for hydraulic equipment, motors, and pumps.
